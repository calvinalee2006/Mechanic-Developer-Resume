Hello my name is Courtney Re Lee and for the last 6 years I have enjoyed providing my professional assistance toward furthering the education of middle school students.

About me

I was born and raised in Junction City Kansas, and currently reisde in my home town with my husband and two children. I enjoy reading novels, crafting, and playing with my children in my spare time. When I find out a show has been made from a novel I have read, I like to torture my husband and tell him all the missing parts of the show that the writers screwed up and/or got wrong.

Teaching

Teaching has been a wonderful profession, I enjoy coming to work and teaching them about literature and helping them further thier education and sometimes helping them go into a career path or their choosing. Teaching has taught me to try and understand students and help them with not only education, but also with personal development. I enjoy talking and laughing with my students and I have personally learned that if I understand them they feel less distant in my classroom and want to engage in classroom activity.
